üç™ What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their site is being used.

Cookies may be either "persistent" cookies or "session" cookies. A persistent cookie remains on your device for a specified period of time and is activated each time you visit the website that created that particular cookie. A session cookie is temporary and is deleted when you close your browser.

Similar technologies like web beacons, pixels, and local storage can also be used for these purposes. In this policy, we refer to all of these technologies as "cookies."
